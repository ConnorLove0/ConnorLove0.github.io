5 Facts About Sushi

  1. Sushi did not originate in Japan, It was actually created in southeast Asia!

  2. Real Wasabi Is Expensive!

  3. Sushi Rice was once considered trash.

  4. The word sushi refers to Rice.

  5. An Earthquake made Sushi more popular. Sushi was traditionally seen as street food and many vendors could not afford to open brick-and-mortar locations. This all changed after there was an earthquake in 1923. The prices of property dipped so low that it was suddenly attainable to purchase a restaurant and serve sushi there.
