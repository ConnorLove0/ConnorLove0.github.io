5 Facts About Apples

  1. There are over 2,500 Varieties of Apples are Grown in the United States

  2. The science of apple growing is called pomology.

  3. Apples will ripen six to ten times faster at room temperature than if they were refrigerated.

  4. It takes about 36 apples to create one gallon of apple cider.

  5. Pilgrims planted the first U.S. apple trees in the Massachusetts Bay Colony.
